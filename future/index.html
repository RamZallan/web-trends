2016 HTLA Senior Project

Virtual Reality


The following site is a proof-of-concept of how sites may look in the future, with Virtual Reality becoming more and more popular. The site is best viewed using Google Cardboard, but it can be viewed using a computer or smartphone. The site isn't interactive; it's comprised of various images creating a mockup site for my high school, High Tech LA. Though the actual graphics may not showcase how sites may look, this proof-of-concept focuses more on the functionality of VR websites that may become more popular in the coming years.
